Introduction

Freenom is a website where anyone can grab a free domain name. A domain name is what's used when visiting a website.

Example: https://facebook.com

Or https://isak-tech.tk

Most domains cost some amount of money to obtain but with Freenom you can get one without cost. So why not right?

Why are .tk domains free?

.tk is the Internet country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Tokelau, a territory of New Zealand in the South Pacific. Source - Wikipedia

Since the demand for .tk domains is so low you can get most of them for free.
